Escape the Crowd: Why Pondok Peneng Is Bali's Best-Kept Highland Retreat

March 3, 2026 · 3 min read

Most travelers picture Bali as beaches, surf, and bustling night markets. But tucked away in the highlands of Baturiti, Tabanan, there's a quieter, greener Bali waiting to be discovered — and Pondok Peneng sits right at the heart of it.

Nestled in the village of Mekarsari, Pondok Peneng is a serene retreat surrounded by rice paddies, blooming flower gardens, and cool mountain air. It's the kind of place where you wake up to birdsong instead of traffic, and spend your evenings watching mist roll across the hills.

A Traditional Home in a Balinese Landscape

The villas at Pondok Peneng are built in the style of a traditional Javanese joglo, blending rustic charm with thoughtful comfort. Guests enjoy:

  • Private cabins with mountain and garden views
  • A shared plunge pool — one of the few in the area at this price point
  • A working rice paddy field and flower garden
  • Shared kitchen and dining area
  • Ample parking for up to 4 cars

Breathtaking Views Without the Premium Price

Starting from just $22 per night, Pondok Peneng offers sweeping highland scenery and an authentic rural atmosphere without the luxury price tag. Whether you're a couple seeking a romantic escape, a digital nomad needing a calm workspace with Wi-Fi, or a nature lover ready to breathe fresh mountain air, this retreat delivers.

Prime Location for Exploring North Tabanan

Staying here puts you minutes away from some of Bali's most rewarding highland destinations:

  • Secret Garden Village — 5 minutes
  • Leke Leke Waterfall — 8 minutes
  • Cocoa Land Bali — 4 minutes
  • Bedugul area and Pura Ulun Danu Beratan — 15 minutes
  • Convenience store — 3 minutes away

Ready to Escape?

Traditional Javanese-style villas, plunge pool, rice-field views — from $22/night in the Bali highlands.

7 Best Things to Do Near Baturiti, Bali

March 3, 2026 · 4 min read

While most tourists flock to Seminyak and Ubud, Baturiti offers some of Bali's most unspoiled highland scenery. Cooler, greener, and far less crowded, it's the Bali many travelers wish they'd discovered sooner. If you're staying at Pondok Peneng in Mekarsari, all of these are right on your doorstep.

  1. Secret Garden Village (5 minutes). An eco-cultural park where visitors explore Bali's agricultural heritage through chocolate-making workshops, traditional plant gardens, and local craft demonstrations. Perfect for families and curious travelers.
  2. Leke Leke Waterfall (8 minutes). Hidden deep in the jungle, Leke Leke is one of Bali's most dramatic hidden waterfalls. The trek is short but immersive, winding through dense forest to a stunning cascade. Wear good shoes and embrace getting a little wet.
  3. Cocoa Land Bali (4 minutes). A hands-on cacao experience where you follow the journey from bean to chocolate bar, complete with guided tours and tastings. A fun stop for all ages.
  4. Danau Beratan & Pura Ulun Danu Beratan (15 minutes). The famous water temple that appears to float on Lake Beratan's surface is one of Bali's most iconic images. Arrive early in the morning when mist still clings to the lake for the most magical atmosphere.
  5. Jatiluwih Rice Terraces (30 minutes). A UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most breathtaking landscapes in Southeast Asia. Go at sunrise or late afternoon for golden-hour views over the cascading rice fields.
  6. Pura Luhur Batukaru (35 minutes). One of Bali's six directional temples, nestled at the foot of Mount Batukaru in an ancient, mist-covered forest. A deeply spiritual and atmospheric experience away from the tourist crowds.
  7. Warung Mina Bedugul (8 minutes). A beloved local seafood warung with generous portions, authentic Balinese flavors, and a setting that makes every meal feel like an occasion.

Tips Before You Go

Rent a scooter or car to explore because public transport is limited. Pack a light jacket since Baturiti evenings are cooler than southern Bali. Visit April to October for the clearest mountain views.

Make the Highlands Your Base

All 7 of these destinations are within 35 minutes of Pondok Peneng — traditional villas from $22/night.

Our Story

Welcome to Pondok Peneng

Nestled in Mekarsari, Baturiti — Pondok Peneng is a traditional Javanese home compound surrounded by lush rice paddy fields, flower gardens, and the gentle sounds of nature.

Just 300 meters from the main road, yet completely immersed in the Balinese countryside. Our five unique villas offer a simple, authentic rural experience — perfect for couples, families, and seekers of true quiet.

Whether you're strolling through rice paddies, watching the morning mist roll off the mountains, or simply sitting in our bamboo gazebo with a cup of Balinese coffee — time slows down here.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where exactly is Pondok Peneng located?

Pondok Peneng is located in Mekarsari, Baturiti, Bali — just 300 meters from the main road, easily accessible by car or motorbike. We're 5 minutes from Secret Garden Village, 8 minutes from Leke Leke Waterfall, and about 1 hour from Ngurah Rai International Airport.

How many people can stay at Pondok Peneng?

Each villa accommodates different group sizes. Villa Siki sleeps up to 4 guests (2 bedrooms), Villa Kalih sleeps 2 (1 bedroom), and Villas Telu, Papat, and Limo are intimate private retreats. The entire compound can host multiple groups simultaneously.

Is there a pool at Pondok Peneng?

Yes! Pondok Peneng features a shared plunge pool available 24 hours a day for all villa guests. It's one of the few properties in the Baturiti area with a pool.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is available from 2:00 PM. Check-out is by 12:00 PM (noon). Our staff is on-site from 8am to 5pm — please let us know your expected arrival time in advance.

How do I book a villa at Pondok Peneng?

All villas are bookable through Airbnb. Click "Book on Airbnb" on any villa card above to check availability and pricing. Host Aryo has a 100% response rate and typically responds within one hour.
